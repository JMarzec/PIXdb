Data availability

Per-dataset

Processed, quality control-approved and normalised data from individual datasets are available from figshare datasets collection.

Per-platform

Combined data from individual RNA-seq and microarray platforms are available from figshare platforms collection.

Cross-platform

Cross-platform integrative analysis results from biological comparisons performed between various prostate cancer stages are available from figshare cross-platform collection.

Stromal genes

The list of consistently deregulated stroma-specific genes is available for downlod from here and was compiled from the following three resources:

1. 869-gene stroma signature from study by Stuart et al. (2004)
2. 139 stromal genes used by ESTIMATE
3. 179 stromal genes from study by Gregg et al. (2010)

Stromal genes were filtered out prior to data integration for a more reliable prostate-specific signature.
